A Hearty Welcome to IIT Delhi!

A huge congratulations on your monumental achievement! Clearing the JEE, one of the world's most challenging examinations, and securing a place at the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi, the #1 engineering college in India, is a testament to your incredible hard work, dedication, and brilliance. This is a moment of immense pride, and we, your seniors at DAAN, are thrilled to welcome you into the IITD family.

As you embark on this exciting new chapter, know that you have a dedicated support system in us. We are here to guide you, support you, and ensure your transition into college life is as smooth and enriching as possible.
